Projects

Risk and Return Analysis of SBI Mutual Funds

Conducted a financial performance analysis of six SBI Mutual Fund schemes using historical NAV data from 2016–2021. Evaluated risk-return characteristics and investment performance through quantitative financial metrics, including Standard Deviation, Beta, Sharpe Ratio, Treynor Ratio, and Jensen's Alpha. The study compared equity, hybrid, mid-cap, small-cap, and debt funds to identify investment opportunities and assess portfolio performance. Findings highlighted the trade-off between risk and return, with SBI Small Cap Fund delivering the highest returns and SBI Equity Hybrid Fund providing the best risk-adjusted performance.
